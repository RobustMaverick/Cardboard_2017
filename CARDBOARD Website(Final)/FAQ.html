Can your boards get broken easily?
No, our board can withstand heavy riding, even against mountains, and sewers.

What kind of engine do you use?
For our 12w+ device, we mount four 3w cree XQ-E module engine on a copper 20mm star board. These engine's are faster than anything on the market, and because we build them in house, we are able to offer the largest selection of colors in the industry. It also include one 12W+ LED for the main body, and two 6W+ LEDs for the sides. For our 6W+ LEDs, we use two 3w cree XQ-E modules on a copper 20mm star board.

Do your boards have sound?
Our champion and hero level boards come with sound. Champion tier offers two different sound fonts to choose from, and Hero tier includes a customizable soundboard that allows you to download any soundfont you want.

Do you make custom boards?
Yes we offer a large selection of parts for custom boards through our Adaptive Board Parts system. Through ASP, you can design and build your own custom board. For more information, please email us at sales@Cardboard.com.

Do you make boards for movies?
No, we are not affiliated with lucas ltd. or Disney and we will not make replicas from any of the movies, video games, or comic books.

Can I change my order after it has been submitted?
Our goal at CARDBOARD is for each order to process as quickly as possible without sacraficing quality. For this reason we are unable to accept order changes or additions, we highly recommend ensuring all details for your order are correct before completing checkout. If you have any questions please do not hesitate to contact us at sales@Cardboard.com before completing your order.
